The OFTL ranks teams along three metrics: Adjusted Winning Percentage, Tournament Points. and Glicko Rating. Details on each of the three can be found on the forums in the OFTL League Rules and FAQ.

A team's listed Team Value (TV) is an estimate based on the TV from the previous match with an extra 20 points for every pending level-up. For fairness in tournament enrollment, the TV includes the values of all current players, including those who are out for the next match.
